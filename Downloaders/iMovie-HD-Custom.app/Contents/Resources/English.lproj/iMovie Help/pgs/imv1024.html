Changing an existing transition or title

You can make changes to transitions and titles that have already been rendered.

To change a transition or title:

  1. Do one of the following:
    • To change a title, select the clip or clips that contain the title you want to change in the clip viewer or timeline viewer.
    • To change a transition, select the transition's icon (shown above) in the clip viewer or timeline viewer.

    Tip: If you want to select all your transitions at once, select a transition and choose Edit > Select Similar Clips.

  2. Click the Editing button, and then click Titles or Transitions at the top of the Editing pane.
  3. Make your modifications in the pane you selected.
  4. Click Update.

If you need to make the same change to more than one clip, select all the clips and update them all at once to save some time. For information about selecting multiple clips, see Related Topics below.
