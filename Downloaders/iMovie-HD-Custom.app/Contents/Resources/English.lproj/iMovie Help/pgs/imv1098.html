Build your movie

4 of 13 >

Your imported footage appears in iMovie as separate clips, or small segments of video. You can crop clips to show only the portion of video you like, and then string them together to create the scenes in your movie.

To build your movie, you just drag the clips to the clip viewer.

Once you've added the clips to your movie, you can drag them into any order you want.
