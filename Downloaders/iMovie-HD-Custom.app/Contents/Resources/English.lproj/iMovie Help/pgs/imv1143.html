I see a message about files that don't belong in my project

If you have files in your iMovie HD project that aren't being used, you'll see a message when you open your project. You can then open the iMovie Trash to see the files and drag them into your project or permanently delete them.

Unused files are usually placed in the iMovie Trash when you import clips into a project and then close the project without saving it, or when iMovie HD unexpectedly quits or is forced to quit while rendering a file.

After you open the Trash, drag needed files back into the Clips pane or permanently delete the files by selecting them and clicking Delete Selected Clips.
