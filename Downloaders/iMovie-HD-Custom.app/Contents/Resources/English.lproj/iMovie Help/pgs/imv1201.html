Moviemaking made easy

3 of 13 >

If you want, you can let the "Magic iMovie" feature automatically create a movie for you.

Magic iMovie can import your footage from a DV or HDV camcorder via FireWire and assemble it into a movie, complete with a title, transitions between scenes, and even a music track of your choosing. Use Magic iMovie to create your whole movie or give you a head start in creating your masterpiece. You can even automatically export your movie to iDVD as a project you can burn to a DVD disc.
