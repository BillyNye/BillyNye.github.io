Opening iMovie HD and starting a project

When you open iMovie HD, you can choose whether to create a new project, open an existing project, or make a Magic iMovie.

To create a new movie or open an existing one:

  1. Open iMovie HD.
  2. Click the button for the option you want:
    • Click "Create a New Project" to create a new movie project into which you can import video.
    • Click "Open an Existing Project" to open a movie that you've been working on.
    • Click "Make a Magic iMovie" to allow iMovie HD to automatically import the video from a camcorder connected by a FireWire cable and compose a movie for you.

If you're new to iMovie, you can open a printable document that takes you step-by-step through the process of making your first movie. To get started, click "iMovie HD Getting Started."
