Working in multiple iMovie HD projects at a time

You can copy individual video and audio clips, and even a whole movie or portion of a movie (including any transitions, titles, and effects), from one iMovie HD project to another.

  1. Choose File > Open to open the projects you want to share video and audio between.

    2. Note: You can have up to ten iMovie HD projects open at once.

  2. Drag files you want to share from one project to another. You can also copy and paste items between projects.

Note: You can only transfer video to or from your camcorder in one iMovie HD project at a time. While your video is transferring, you cannot work in any other iMovie HD projects you have open. Working in multiple projects at a time requires a good deal of memory and may affect the performance of iMovie HD.
