I can't apply pan and zoom effects to a still image

You can apply pan and zoom effects to most image files that are in JPEG or TIFF format, but not to still images you create from digital video.

If you can't add pan and zoom to an image from your iPhoto library, make sure the Ken Burns Effect checkbox is selected in the Photo Settings window. (Note: The Photo Settings window opens when you select the photo.)
