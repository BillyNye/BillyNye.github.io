My iSight won't communicate with iMovie HD

If you can't get your iSight to communicate with iMovie HD, try these quick troubleshooting steps:

  • Make sure your iSight cable is firmly connected to the FireWire port (not USB) on your computer.
  • Make sure the lens is open.
  • Turn off any other applications that use iSight (such as iChat AV).
  • Choose Apple menu > About This Mac to verify that you have Mac OS X version 10.3.9 or later installed. (Mac OS X version 10.4.4 or later is recommended to take full advantage of iMovie HD's features.)
  • If no microphone is detected, and the error messages don't seem to match your particular situation, disconnect iSight and reconnect it. You might also check your audio input settings (open System Preferences, click Sound, and then click Input).
  • Restart your computer and try again.

For more information about iSight, go to the iSight support website.
