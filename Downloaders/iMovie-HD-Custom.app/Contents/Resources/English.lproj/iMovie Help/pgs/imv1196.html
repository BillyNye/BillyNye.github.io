Sending your movie as an email attachment

You can email a compressed, QuickTime version of your movie to family and friends.

To send your movie as an email attachment:

  1. Choose Share > Email.
  2. Choose the email application you want to use from the pop-up menu.
  3. If you want, type a new name for the movie you're sending.
  4. If you only want to share clips you selected, click the "Share selected clips only" checkbox.
  5. Click Share.

    6. iMovie opens a New Message window in your email application with the QuickTime movie attached.

  6. Enter an address, a subject line, and the text of the message, then click Send.

IMPORTANT: If you send a movie that has a large file size, it will take longer for people to download it. It's a good idea to limit the size of the movie you send.
