Restoring a clip to its original state

If you decide you want to start over after making changes to a clip, you can revert to its original version. iMovie discards all the changes you made since you imported the clip into your project.

To restore a clip to its original state:

  1. Select the clip in the Clips pane, timeline viewer, or clip viewer.
  2. Choose Advanced > "Revert Clip to Original."

The "Revert Clip to Original" command is dimmed if you haven't made any changes to the clip.

You can also undo your most recent changes by choosing Edit > Undo.
