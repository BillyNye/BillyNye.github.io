Enhance the action with sound

8 of 13 >

You can adjust the volume of the audio that's already in your video footage, or give your movie a professional flare by adding sound effects, music, and even your own voiceover. Bring in music directly from your iTunes library, or score your own soundtrack right in GarageBand.

Use the graphic equalizer in iMovie HD to remove wind noise or camera hum, or to adjust the bass or treble in a particular video or audio clip. You can also change the pitch of your audio and add reverb, delay, and other audio effects to make your movies sound better than ever.
