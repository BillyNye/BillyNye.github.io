Viewing a clip's duration and other information

iMovie stores information about each video and audio clip in your project, including its name, file type and size, the date it was captured, and its duration.

To view a clip's duration and other information:

Select the clip in the Clips pane, clip viewer, or timeline viewer, and then choose File > Show Info.

Tip: You can also double-click a clip to show its information.
