Playing your movie so that it fills the whole computer screen

To better see the details in your movie, you can play it so that it fills the whole computer screen. This is called "full-screen playback."

To use full-screen playback:

  1. Click the Play Full Screen button (shown above).
  2. Click anywhere in the movie to stop it from playing and leave full-screen view.
