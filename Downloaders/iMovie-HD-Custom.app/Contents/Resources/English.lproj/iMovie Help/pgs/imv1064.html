Importing video from another iMovie HD project

You can copy individual video and audio clips, and even a whole movie or a portion of a movie (including any transitions, titles, and effects), from one iMovie HD project to another.

To copy video between iMovie HD projects:

  1. Open the project from which you want to copy clips or a portion of your movie.
  2. Select the clips and choose Edit > Copy.
  3. Open the iMovie HD project that you want to copy into.
  4. Choose Edit > Paste.

You can also drag clips from one iMovie HD project directly to the Clips pane, clip viewer, or timeline viewer in another iMovie HD project.
