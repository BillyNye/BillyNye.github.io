I get feedback from my computer speakers when I connect my camcorder

You hear feedback from your computer speakers because the microphone on your camcorder picks up what's coming out of the speakers and plays it back through the speakers. You can avoid this problem in a couple of ways:

  • Move the camcorder away from the computer until the feedback stops.
  • Move the volume slider below the iMovie monitor all the way to the left to mute the sound.
  • If you have an Apple keyboard with volume keys, press the Mute key.
