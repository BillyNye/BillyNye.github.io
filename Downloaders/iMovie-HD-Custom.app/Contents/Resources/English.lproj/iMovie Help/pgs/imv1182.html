Changing the order of clips in a movie

  1. Click the Clip Viewer button (shown above).
  2. Drag clips into the order you want in the clip viewer.

You can also change the order of clips in the timeline viewer. To show the timeline viewer, click the Timeline Viewer button (a clock) next to the Clip Viewer button. When moving a clip in the timeline, first drag it downward to remove it from its location.

Tip: You can also drag several clips at once or move clips around by cutting and pasting. For more information, see Related Topics.
