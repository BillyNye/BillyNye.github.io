Changing the display duration of a photo

After adding a photo to your movie, you can change its display duration at any time.

The duration of the clip is shown on the clip's border (in minutes:seconds:frames) when you view it in the Clips pane or clip viewer.

To change the display duration of a photo:

  1. Select the photo in the clip viewer or timelime viewer.
  2. If you don't see the Photo Settings window, click the Media button, click Photos at the top of the Media pane, and then click Show Photo Settings.
  3. In the Photo Settings window, drag the duration control (shown below) to specify a new duration in seconds and frames.

Tip: If you didn't add a pan and zoom effect to your photo, you can quickly change its display duration by double-clicking it, typing a new duration in the Duration field, and clicking Set.

Tip: You can also adjust the display duration of a still image by dragging one of its edges in the timeline viewer.
