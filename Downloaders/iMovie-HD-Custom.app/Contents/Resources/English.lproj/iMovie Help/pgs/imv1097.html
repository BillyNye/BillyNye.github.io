Importing video is a snap

2 of 13 >

iMovie HD supports importing a variety of video formats, including DV, widescreen DV, high definition (HDV), MPEG-4 (Simple Profile), and more. You can also capture live video with your iSight (including the built-in iSight on your iMac) and send it directly to iMovie HD.

If you have video or audio clips stored on your computer, you can drag them into an iMovie HD project from your desktop. You can even drag video and audio clips from one iMovie HD project to another!
