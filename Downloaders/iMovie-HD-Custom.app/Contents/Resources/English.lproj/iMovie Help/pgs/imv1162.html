Turning the recording level of a microphone up or down

If the sound you're recording with a microphone is too loud or too soft, you can change the recording level in the Sound pane of System Preferences.

To change the recording level of your microphone:

  1. Open System Preferences, and then click Sound.
  2. Click Input.
  3. Adjust the "Input volume" slider to increase or decrease the recording volume.
