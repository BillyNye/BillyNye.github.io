Splitting a video or audio clip

You can divide or "split" a video clip so that you can insert titles, insert other clips or images, or reorder the sequence of clips. You can split clips that are in the Clips pane, clip viewer, or timeline viewer. You can also split audio clips in the timeline viewer.

To split a video or audio clip:

  1. Select the clip you want to split in the Clips pane, clip viewer, or timeline viewer.
  2. Position the playhead where you want to divide the clip.

    3. Tip: To precisely adjust the playhead, press the Left or Right Arrow key to move it one frame at a time. To move the playhead in ten-frame increments, hold down the Shift key while pressing the arrow key.

  3. Choose Edit > "Split Video Clip at Playhead" or "Split Selected Audio Clip at Playhead."

If the command is not available in the Edit menu, make sure that you've selected a clip and that the playhead is located within the clip.
