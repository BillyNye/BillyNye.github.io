Deleting unwanted files and footage

You can remove unwanted audio or video clips from your movie by deleting them or dragging them to the iMovie Trash. The iMovie Trash holds the deleted clips until you empty it. You can free hard disk space by emptying the iMovie Trash, or you can recover files by dragging them out of the iMovie Trash again.

WARNING: After you empty the iMovie Trash, you cannot undo any of the changes you made to your project before that time.

To delete footage:

  1. Select the audio or video clips you want to delete.

    2. To learn how to select multiple clips, see Related Topics below.

  2. Choose Edit > Clear or press the Delete key.

To see the contents of the iMovie Trash, click the Trash icon, or choose File > Show Trash.

To empty the iMovie Trash and free up disk space, choose File > Empty Trash or click the Empty Trash button in the iMovie Trash window.

Note: If you import video clips and then quit iMovie HD without saving your project, the clips are placed in the iMovie Trash. The next time you open the project you can retrieve the clips from the Trash or delete them permanently.
