Creating a black or color clip

You can create a black or color clip to use in your movie. For example, you may want to separate scenes in your movie by dipping to black, or create a color background for some titles or credits.

To create a black clip:

  1. Click the Timeline Viewer button (shown above).
  2. Drag a video clip in the timeline viewer to the left or right.

    3. A gap appears at one end of the clip.

  3. Click the Clip Viewer button (shown below).

    4. The gap is represented by a black clip.

When you play your movie, the gap you created appears as black, silent video.

To change a black clip to color, select the clip, choose File > Show Info, click the Color box, and choose a color for the clip.

You can work with black or color clips as you would with any other clips, adding titles, changing the clip's duration, and so on.
