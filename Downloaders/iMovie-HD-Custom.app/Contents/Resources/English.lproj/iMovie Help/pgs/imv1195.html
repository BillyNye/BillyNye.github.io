Publishing your movie to the web using iWeb

iWeb is an application that lets you create and publish your own websites. You can transfer a movie directly from iMovie to iWeb to create a webpage, blog, or podcast for your movie, and then publish it to the web.

Note: To publish your movie to the web using iWeb, you must have iWeb 1.0 or later installed on your computer.

To publish your movie to the web:

  1. Choose Share > iWeb.
  2. Do one of the following:
    • To share your movie as a blog or podcast, click "Share for video podcast."
    • To send your movie to a Movie page, click "Share for web."
  3. If you only want to share clips you selected, select the "Share selected clips only" checkbox.
  4. Click Share.

    5. Your movie is saved as a QuickTime movie and opens in iWeb.

For more information about using iWeb to design and publish your movie's webpage, choose Help > iWeb Help when iWeb is open.
