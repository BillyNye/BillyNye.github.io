Playing a clip

After you import a clip or make changes to it, it's a good idea to play it to determine whether additional changes need to be made.

Here are ways to play clips:

  • To play an entire clip, select the clip and click the Play button (shown above) or press the Space bar.
  • To stop a clip from playing, click the Play button or press the Space bar again.
  • To quickly move to a new location in a clip, drag the playhead (shown below) to a new location in the scrubber bar, or click in the scrubber bar to jump to that location.
  • To move through a clip one frame at a time, press the Left or Right Arrow key while the clip isn't playing. You can also hold down the arrow keys to scan through a clip frame by frame.
  • To move through a clip ten frames at a time, hold down the Shift key while pressing the arrow keys. You can also hold down both the Shift and arrow keys to scan through a clip in ten-frame intervals.

You can also select and play multiple clips at once. For more information, see Related Topics below.
