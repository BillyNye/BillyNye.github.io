Share your movie

12 of 13 >

When your iMovie is finished, you can view it full-screen on your computer in iMovie HD; use iDVD to burn it on a DVD disc for viewing with most standard DVD players; transfer it to your iPod; or email it to a friend.

You can use iWeb to publish your movie to the web as a video podcast or movie webpage. You can also send your movie wirelessly to a Bluetooth device such as a compatible mobile phone, copy it back to a tape in your camcorder and then view it on your TV, or burn the project files to a DVD disc so that others can view or edit your project in iMovie HD.
