The video from my camcorder doesn't appear in the iMovie monitor

If you don't see video from your camcorder in the iMovie monitor, try the following:

  • Make sure the camcorder is set to VTR (playback) mode. (Some camcorders call this VCR or Play.)
  • Make sure your camcorder is turned on and the FireWire cable is securely attached to the camcorder (or the camcorder's docking system) and to your computer.
  • Make sure your camcorder and computer are connected via the FireWire ports, not the USB ports. Some camcorders label the FireWire port "iLink."
  • Disconnect the FireWire cable from your computer and then reconnect it.
  • Turn your camcorder off and then on again.
  • Check to see if you have the correct tape in the camcorder and the tape is queued up to the right part.
