Improving audio-recording quality

You can set up your camera to record higher-quality audio, which will improve the audio quality of your movies. Check the manual that came with your DV camcorder to find out how to set the audio format.

For best results when creating long movies (over 30 minutes), record footage using 16-bit audio. Recording with 12-bit audio may result in a gradual loss of audio-video synchronization.
