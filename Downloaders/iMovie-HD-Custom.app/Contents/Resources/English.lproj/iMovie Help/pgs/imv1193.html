Layering multiple audio clips in a single track

You can place multiple audio clips on top of each other in a single audio track. For example, you might want to place a sound effect over your narration or a song, or blend together two sound effects. iMovie automatically mixes the layered sounds and plays them simultaneously.

To layer multiple audio clips in a single track:

Drag or paste an audio clip onto an existing clip in one of the audio tracks.
