My converter box won't work with iMovie HD

If you're using an analog to digital (A/D) converter box to connect a non-digital camera to iMovie HD, you may have to turn the converter box power off and on several times (this is called "cycling the power").

If you're still having problems, change the input on the converter box one or more times to see if this solves the problem.
