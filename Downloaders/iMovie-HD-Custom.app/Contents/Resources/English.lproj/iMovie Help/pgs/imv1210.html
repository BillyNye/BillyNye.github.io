Transferring your movie to an iPod

You can transfer a movie to your iPod by exporting it from iMovie HD to your iTunes library, and then using iTunes to copy the movie to your iPod.

IMPORTANT: To export a movie from iMovie HD to your iPod, you must have an iPod with video capability. You must also have QuickTime 7.0.4 or later and iTunes 6.0 or later installed on your computer. For the latest versions of these applications, open System Preferences, click Software Update, and then click Check Now.

To transfer your movie to an iPod:

  1. In iMovie HD, open the movie you want to transfer.

    2. If you only want to transfer a portion of your movie, select the clips you want to transfer in the timeline viewer.

  2. Choose Share > iPod.
  3. Click the "Share selected clips only" checkbox if you only want to share clips you selected.
  4. Click Share.

    5. iMovie HD compresses your movie and saves it to your iTunes library. The time it takes to save the movie depends on the movie's length.

  5. Open iTunes 6.0 or later, and then sync with your iPod.

    6. For more information about syncing, see the documentation that came with your video-compatible iPod.
