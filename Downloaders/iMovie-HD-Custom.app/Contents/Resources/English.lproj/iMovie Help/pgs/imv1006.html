I can't control my camcorder with the iMovie HD capture controls

Make sure that your camcorder is connected correctly with a FireWire cable, that it's turned on and has power, that it's in VTR, VCR, or Play mode, and that the tape is rewound in the camcorder. The iMovie monitor should say "Camera Connected."

Last, make sure you click the mode switch to set iMovie HD to camera mode:

If you connect a non-DV device, such as a media converter box, you may need to use the playback controls on the camera instead of the ones in iMovie HD.
