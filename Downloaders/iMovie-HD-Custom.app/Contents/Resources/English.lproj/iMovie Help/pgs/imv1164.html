Which video formats are compatible with iMovie HD?

iMovie HD supports importing video in common formats, such as standard digital video formats like NTSC and PAL, high definition video formats (HDV 720p and 1080i), and MPEG-4, 16:9 widescreen format, and clips from Apple's iSight camera.

iMovie HD also allows you to export your movie in the most commonly used video formats for playing or editing on other video devices. If you have a video project that has a special requirement, you can export your movie using expert settings.
