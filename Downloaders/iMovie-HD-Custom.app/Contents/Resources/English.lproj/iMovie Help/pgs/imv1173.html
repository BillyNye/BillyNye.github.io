Listening to your audio as you move the playhead

To help align sound with video, you can play the audio tracks as you drag the playhead along the timeline viewer.

To listen to your audio as you move the playhead:

Hold down the Option key as you drag the playhead in the timeline viewer.

Tip: You can also hear sound as you change the length of an audio clip. To do so, hold down the Option key as you drag the end of a clip to trim it.
