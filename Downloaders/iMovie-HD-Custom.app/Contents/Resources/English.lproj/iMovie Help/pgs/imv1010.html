Playing video simultaneously in iMovie HD and on your camcorder

You can play your DV project simultaneously on your DV camcorder and in the iMovie monitor.

Note: You cannot use this feature with high definition video, MPEG-4, or iSight projects. Some operations, such as previewing effects, transitions, themes, and titles, will work more slowly when this feature is enabled.

To play video simultaneously in iMovie HD and on your camera:

  1. Choose iMovie HD > Preferences.
  2. Click Playback.
  3. Select "Play DV project video through to DV camera."
