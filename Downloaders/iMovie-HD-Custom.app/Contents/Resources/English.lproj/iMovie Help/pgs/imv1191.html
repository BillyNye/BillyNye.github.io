Removing transitions, titles, and effects

You can easily remove any transition, title, or effect that you've added to your movie.

To remove a transition, title, or effect:

  1. Do one of the following:
    • To remove a title or effect, select the clip or clips that contain the title or effect you want to remove in the clip viewer or timeline viewer.
    • To remove a transition, select the transition's icon (shown above) in the clip viewer or timeline viewer.

    Tip: If you want to select all your transitions at once, select a transition and choose Edit > Select Similar Clips.

  2. Press the Delete key.

Note: When you remove a title or effect from a clip, you restore the clip to how it looked before you added the title or effect. If a clip was split in two when you added the title to it, iMovie merges the clips back together when the title is removed.
