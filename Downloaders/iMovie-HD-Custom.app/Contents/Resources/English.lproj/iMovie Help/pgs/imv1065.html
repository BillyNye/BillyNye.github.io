Removing the audio when pasting over a video clip

When you paste new video over an existing video clip in iMovie HD, you can make the audio in the new video clip replace the existing audio.

To remove the audio when pasting over a video clip:

  1. Choose iMovie HD > Preferences.
  2. Click General.
  3. Deselect the "Extract audio when using Paste Over At Playhead" checkbox.
