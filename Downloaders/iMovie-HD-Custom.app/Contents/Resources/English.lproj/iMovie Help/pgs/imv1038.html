Adjusting the volume of your computer in iMovie HD

As you work on a movie, you can turn the volume of your computer up or down to better hear the playback.

IMPORTANT: Adjusting the volume of your computer in iMovie HD does not affect the sound others will hear when your movie is played. To learn how to make volume adjustments within your movie's video and audio clips, see Related Topics below.

To change the volume of your computer in iMovie HD:

Drag the Volume control (shown above).

The volume level of your computer is set in the Sound pane of System Preferences. To increase or decrease the overall range of sound volume, open System Preferences and change the output volume.
