Backing up a project to DVD disc

You can burn a copy of your iMovie HD project to a writeable DVD disc. Most projects will require the full amount of disc space provided by a DVD. Before you burn your project to DVD, make sure the size of your project does not exceed the amount of disc space provided by the DVD.

You can burn your project on DVD-R, DVD-RW, DVD+R, and DVD+RW discs as long as your computer supports the DVD format you choose.

The backup copy includes all the media files, or "assets," used in the project. You can then transfer the project and its files to another computer, keep the disc for safekeeping, or use the disc later to restore the project onto your computer for further editing.

Note: When you back up a project on DVD, you can't play the DVD in a DVD player or QuickTime player.

To back up a project to DVD disc:

  1. Choose File > "Burn Project to Disc."
  2. Confirm that you want to create an archive of your project.
  3. Insert a blank DVD and click Burn.
