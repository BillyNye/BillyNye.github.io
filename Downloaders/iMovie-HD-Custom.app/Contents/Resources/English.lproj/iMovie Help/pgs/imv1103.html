Add your favorite photos

9 of 13 >

Have photos you'd love to include in your movie? You can add pictures directly from your iPhoto library.

You can also add a drawing, background graphic, or most any other kind of image file by dragging it directly into iMovie. And after you've added an image to your movie, you can add motion to it by panning across the image or by zooming in or out.
