Choosing where imported clips appear

You can choose to import new video clips into the Clips pane or into the timeline viewer and clip viewer.

To choose where imported clips appear:

  1. Choose iMovie HD > Preferences.
  2. Click Import.
  3. Click Clips Pane or Movie Timeline.

If you click Movie Timeline, the clips appear in the timeline viewer and in the clip viewer.
