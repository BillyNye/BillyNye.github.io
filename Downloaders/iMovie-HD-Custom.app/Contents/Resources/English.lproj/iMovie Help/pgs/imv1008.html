I hear a sound when importing video

If the video footage you are importing has some audio problems, you hear a loud beep or popping sound while you are importing. You can filter these problems as you import the video.

To clean up audio problems during import:

  1. Choose iMovie HD > Preferences.
  2. Click Import.
  3. Select the "Filter audio from camera" checkbox.
  4. Import your video again.
