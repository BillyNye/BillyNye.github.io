Resizing the iMovie HD window to show the full resolution of your footage

You can adjust the size of your iMovie HD window to be sure your footage is being displayed at its full resolution.

To resize the iMovie HD window:

Choose Window > Show Full Video Resolution.

Note: If the menu command is dimmed, the window is correctly sized to display your video's full resolution size.

If the correct resolution would create a window too large for your screen, iMovie HD only expands the window as large as possible. The "Show Full Video Resolution" command remains highlighted if this is the case.
