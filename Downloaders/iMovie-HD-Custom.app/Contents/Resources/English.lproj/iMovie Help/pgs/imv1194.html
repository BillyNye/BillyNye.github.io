Moving an audio clip

To move an audio clip:

  1. Click the Timeline Viewer button (shown above).
  2. Drag the audio clip to a new location in either audio track.

When you drag an audio clip, the playhead attaches itself to the beginning of the clip. This allows you to watch the iMovie monitor to see which video frame will align with the start of the audio clip. To learn more about precisely aligning audio to video, see Related Topics below.

You can also place multiple audio clips on top of each other in a single audio track. iMovie automatically mixes the layered sounds and plays them simultaneously.
