Adding photos from other locations on your hard disk

In addition to adding photos from your iPhoto library, you can import photos or images into your movie that aren't in your iPhoto application.

Note: iMovie can import any image files that are compatible with QuickTime, including JPEG, GIF, PICT, PSD, and PDF files.

To add photos from other locations on your hard disk:

  1. Choose File > Import.
  2. Select the file you want to import.
  3. Click Open.

You can also drag files from your desktop or from other applications to the Clips pane, the timeline viewer, or the clip viewer. You can also drag, copy, and paste photos between iMovie HD projects.
