Add text to your video

11 of 13 >

You can add text anywhere you want in your movie. Create opening titles, closing credits, or text to identify a person or location in your movie. You can even choose the fonts and text colors you want.

Text can appear over video, over photos, over black, or over a background color or image of your choice. You can even add a photo or other video to play inside the title letters for a really professional look.
