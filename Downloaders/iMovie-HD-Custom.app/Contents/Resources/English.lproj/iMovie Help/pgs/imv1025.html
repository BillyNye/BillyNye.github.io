Adding slow motion or speeding up a clip

If you want a clip or part of your movie to play in slow or fast motion, you can add the Fast/Slow/Reverse effect to it and set how fast you want your selected footage to play.

To learn how to add this effect, see "Using special effects to add visual interest" below.
