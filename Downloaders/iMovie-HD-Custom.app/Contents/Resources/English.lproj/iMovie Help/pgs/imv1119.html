Playing your movie

You can watch your movie from beginning to end, or just play a portion of it, including any transitions, titles, and effects you added. As you build your movie and make changes to it, it's a good idea to play it often to review your progress and determine any additional changes that need to be made.

To play your movie:

  1. Click the Rewind button (A, shown above) to move the playhead to the beginning of a selected portion of video. Click it again to move the playhead to the beginning of the movie.
  2. Click the Play button (B, shown above) or press the Space bar to start playing your movie.
  3. To stop playing your movie, click the Play button or press the Space bar again.

You can also press the Play Full Screen button (C, shown above) to see the movie play full-screen on your computer's monitor. To stop the movie from playing and leave full-screen view, click anywhere in the movie.

To see just a portion of your movie play, select the clip or clips you want to watch and click the Play button or press the Space bar. You can also drag the playhead to any location in your movie and then play from there.
