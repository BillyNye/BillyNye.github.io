The audio is missing from my imported QuickTime movie

If audio is missing from your imported QuickTime movie, you may be importing video in MPEG-1 Muxed format or MPEG-2 Muxed format. Only the video portion is imported from movies in these formats.

To see if your imported movie file is in MPEG-1 or MPEG-2 Muxed format:

  1. Open the original movie file in QuickTime Player.
  2. Choose Window > Show Movie Info.
  3. If necessary, click More Info in the Movie Info window to see the movie format.
