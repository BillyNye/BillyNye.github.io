My microphone doesn't pick up sound

If your computer doesn't pick up sound when you click the Record button in the Audio pane of iMovie HD, there are a couple of things you can try.

If your microphone doesn't pick up sound:

  • Make sure your microphone is selected as the main input and the input volume is not muted. Choose Apple menu > System Preferences, and then click Sound. Then click Input and select your microphone. Make sure the input volume is set high enough to be effective.
  • If an external microphone is connected but it is not picking up sound, check that it is securely connected to the USB port (or microphone port, if applicable) on your computer, and then restart the computer.
