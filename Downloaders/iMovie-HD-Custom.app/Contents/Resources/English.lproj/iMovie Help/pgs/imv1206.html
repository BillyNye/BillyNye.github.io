Scoring your movie in GarageBand

You can open your movie in GarageBand to create an original musical score and synchronize the music with the action in your movie.

To open your movie in GarageBand:

  1. Choose Share > GarageBand.
  2. If you only want to send clips you selected in the timeline viewer or clip viewer, select the "Share selected clips only" checkbox. Don't check this box if you want to send the whole movie.
  3. Click Share.

    4. GarageBand opens and your movie appears in a new project in GarageBand.

For more information about using GarageBand, choose Help > GarageBand Help when GarageBand is open.
